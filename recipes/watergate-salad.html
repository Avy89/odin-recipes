Watergate Salad

Description

When it comes to summer desserts, ice cream often gets all the fame. Ice cream is pretty great, but watergate salad is one of my favorite underated summer desserts. If you've never tried watergate salad because it looks a little strange, you should really give it a shot.

Watergate salad is a perfect combo of textures. At the base you've got pistachio flavored Jell-O and whipped cream. Then, for crunch, you've got some chopped pecans. Finally, you have juicy pineapple and sweet marshmallows that bring in all dimensions of sweetness.
