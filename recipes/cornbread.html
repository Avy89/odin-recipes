Sweet Potato Cornbread

Description

The smell of fresh cornbread triggers deep childhood memories for me. Time travel doesn't exsist yet, but if I close my eyes when smelling cornbread I am back in my grandmother's kitchen. Wether you're cooking a big pot of chili or just want some comfort food on a bad day, cornbread is the answer.

This particular cornbread is unique because it has sweet potatoes in it! The sweet potatoes really help bring extra sweetness and flavor while also helping the cornbread stay moist. It is such a simple and delicious recipe that I'm confident everyone will love.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups + 6 tbsp cornmeal
  • 2 tbsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 3 tbsp sugar
  • 5 lg eggs
  • 2 cups mashed sweet potatoes
  • 8 oz sour cream
  • 8 oz unsalted butter, melted

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 425 F
  2. In a large bowl, mix all of the dry ingredients.
  3. In a seperate bowl, mix all of the wet ingredients.
  4. Slowly pour the dry ingredients into the wet ingredients, stopping to stir frequently.
  5. Pour the batter into a greased cast iron skillet (or 9x9 pan) and bake for 25-35 minutes.
